What performed best

  • 1.Specific numbers — "saved 3 hours" beats "saves time"
  • 2.Real story posts — "I switched to Cursor AI" beats "tools comparison"
  • 3.Actionable takeaways — "do THIS today" beats "here's something interesting"
  • 4.Wednesday posting — consistent day builds audience expectation

What didn't work

  • ✗Generic AI listicles ("10 AI tools you should try") — no differentiation
  • ✗Too long captions on Threads — IG captions don't port perfectly
  • ✗Posting without a hook in the first line — first sentence does 80% of the work
  • ✗Vague CTAs — "follow for more" alone underperforms vs. a specific promise

The 4 lessons I'd apply from day 1

Lesson 01
Build the automation before post #1. Not after post #20.
Lesson 02
Start collecting emails from your first post. The list outlasts every platform.
Lesson 03
Post imperfect content weekly. Not perfect content quarterly.
Lesson 04
Pick one platform and automate it fully before adding a second.
