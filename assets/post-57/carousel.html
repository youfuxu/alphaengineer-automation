My actual workflow

  • 1.Open Claude: "Design the architecture for [feature]. What are the tradeoffs between these approaches?"
  • 2.Claude gives a structured recommendation. I pick a direction.
  • 3.Switch to editor (Cursor with Copilot). Start implementing. Copilot autocompletes the patterns.
  • 4.Hit a bug? Back to Claude with the full error context.

Claude handles the WHY. Copilot handles the WHAT. Both are faster than writing either alone.
